Privacy Policy

ApplyPilot is a local-first career assistant operated by Rishal V S. We may collect your name, email, company, selected plan, license activation details, payment status, and support messages.

Your resume, career profile, job preferences, and application logs are designed to remain on your own machine unless you explicitly connect the desktop app to SaaS sync or export data yourself.

Payment information is processed by the selected payment provider. ApplyPilot does not store card details.

We use customer information to provide licenses, activate devices, process subscriptions, support customers, improve the product, and prevent abuse. We do not sell personal data.
