FAQ

Clear expectations before payment.

Does ApplyPilot need my LinkedIn password?

No. The desktop agent uses the customer’s own browser session. ApplyPilot does not ask customers to share passwords.

Does it submit applications automatically?

Only after the customer enables auto-submit and confirms the run. The default mode is review-only.

Is this only for experienced candidates?

No. ApplyPilot is designed to help new job seekers who may not have a resume yet.

Which platforms are supported?

The current product focuses on local dashboard workflows with LinkedIn/Naukri-oriented job search and application assistance.
