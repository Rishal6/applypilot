Privacy Policy

ApplyPilot is a local-first career assistant operated by Rishal V S. This policy explains what information may be collected when you use the website, checkout, license activation, or desktop agent.

Information we collect

We may collect your name, email address, company name, selected plan, payment status, license activation details, and support messages. Payment information is processed by the payment provider and is not stored by ApplyPilot.

Local career data

Your resume, career profile, job preferences, and application logs are designed to remain on your own machine unless you explicitly connect the desktop app to ApplyPilot SaaS sync or export data yourself.

How we use information

We use information to provide licenses, activate desktop devices, process subscriptions, support customers, improve the product, and prevent abuse.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share limited information with infrastructure, payment, email, and analytics providers only as needed to operate ApplyPilot.

Retention

Billing and license records are kept as long as needed for account, compliance, and support purposes. Local desktop data remains under the customer’s control.
